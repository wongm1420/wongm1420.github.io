June 2026

Honoured to support the final day of a four-day Management Consulting taster programme, where secondary school students worked to answer a challenging question: how can different stakeholder groups contribute towards improving the lives of Hong Kong's elderly? What inspired me most wasn't the final presentations, but the process behind them. Rather than jumping straight into solutions, students spent time speaking directly with ethnic minority elderly members of the community, conducting street interviews and asking thoughtful questions to understand the challenges they face. I witnessed students actively take notes, challenge assumptions, and refine their ideas based on what they learned on the ground. It was a reminder that meaningful solutions rarely come from sitting behind a laptop. They come from immersing yourself in a problem, listening carefully to the people who experience it firsthand, and being willing to have your assumptions challenged. Thank you to Spencer Liu for the invitation and opportunity to contribute, the team at The Zubin Foundation for facilitating such engaging discussions with the elderly, and to OWN Academy for facilitating an engaging week of learning.

May 2026

Grateful to have Prof. David Hagmann join Austin Lee and me for dinner, through HKUST's "Take Faculty to Lunch" (or dinner) program. A wonderful opportunity to know more about professors and their experiences beyond the classroom, discussing our views on everything from careers, geopolitics, and the economy to the lifestyle of academics and the importance of a good mindset. My key takeaway from Prof. Hagmann was to stay in the present; worries about the future are for our future self, and it will work out in the end (evidently, as we all had commitments early next morning!) Highly recommended opportunity for students to sign up and learn more about their faculty!

April 2026

Honoured to emcee at the HSBC Hong Kong x JA Innovation Pop-up this past weekend. Truly a full circle moment for me since I first participated in the Company Program in 2023/24 through Zester Limited. Having spoken to the CEOs and students of many teams, one quality in particular shone through: Resilience. Apart from juggling the usual academics, team dynamics and supplier delays, teams this year were also faced with a different challenge: the fallout of the tragic Tai Po fire. Despite the circumstances, the ways in which they overcame adversity continuously inspired me, finding leads and making sales in innovative ways. They reminded me that with passion, determination and a bit of optimism, every setback is simply a setup for a stronger comeback when approached with the right mindset. Thank you to Vivian Cheuk Wai Choi, Coco Wan and the team at Junior Achievement Hong Kong for orchestrating such a well-run event, and to Aurora ip for partnering with me as emcee. Looking forward to the final awards in April!

September 2024

An eye-opening afternoon at #RethinkHK. From Lufthansa's latest SAF and Sun-to-liquid technology to ADL's eye-catching display of their newest E100EV, and CHOMP's app for saving food waste, it was inspiring to witness how sustainability has found its way into every sector imaginable, and be able to connect with the people behind this momentous undertaking. Thank you to those who took the time to speak with me, and here's to an even bigger Rethink expo next year!
